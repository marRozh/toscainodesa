LIVE
OPERATIC PERFORMANCE

will take place at Holy Trinity Church in Sloane Square, London SW1X 8BZ.

The LIVE OPERATIC PERFORMANCE, a special version of Puccini's TOSCA set in contemporary Odesa during the war.

TOSCA will be staged, combining theatre and opera, with the most beautiful arias of Tosca, in all their unforgettable melodic vein conceived by Puccini, including the immortal arias "Recondita Armonia", "Vissi d'Arte”, “E Lucevan le Stelle” and great moments of Scarpia. The performance is directed and adapted by the director Samuel Mac Fadden.

CHARITIES TO SUPPORT IN UKRAINE

Foundation
Impulsua.org

The foundation was established in 2017 in the Ukrainian city of Zaporizhzhia aiming to support vulnerable children and young individuals. Since the onset of Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, the foundation is focused on helping refugees coming to Zaporizhzhia in significant numbers.

The foundation's dedicated team prioritises aiding displaced women with children and over the course of the war, it helped hundreds of internally displaced families within Zaporizhzhia and the surrounding regions.

This aid encompasses a wide spectrum of services, including humanitarian, social, psychological, and legal assistance.

Since the spring of 2023, the dedicated team of the foundation delivers humanitarian aid to settlements situated on the conflict's frontline. This aid predominantly comprises hygiene and household essentials, as well as crucial medicines tailored for the most vulnerable segments of the population: the elderly and the seriously ill.

Foundation
KHARPP

In collaboration with Kharp, a British charity, we are actively raising funds to support the communities in the Kharkiv Oblast, one of the hardest-hit regions in Ukraine.

Our primary focus lies in the reconstruction of homes, hospitals, schools, and community centres. Specifically, we are dedicated to repairing homes with light to medium-level damage to ensure that families can live there comfortably and warmly, especially with the approaching winter season. Our priority is directed towards vulnerable groups, including the disabled, elderly, households with children, pregnant women, and single mothers.

The average cost of repairing a home, including windows and doors, amounts to £500. With just £5000 raised, we can potentially repair approximately 10 homes, benefiting around 25 people in total.

Once the fundraising is successfully completed, we will showcase a selection of these homes and conduct brief interviews with the residents to share their inspiring stories.

Your support can truly make a significant difference in the lives of those in need in Kharkiv Oblast.

Oksana Hankevich's
Project "Keep Living"

The "Oksana Hankevich Foundation" is a project that aims to bring together women who have lost their husbands and sons while defending Ukraine in the current war.

The foundation empowers these women to become stronger, preserve the memory of their loved ones, and showcase the enduring light of love within them. They hold on not only for themselves but also for the future of Ukraine.

The project's founder, Oksana Hankevich, organizes cultural and artistic events where the wives and mothers of fallen heroes actively participate.

Over time, Oksana Hankevich has launched various projects to instill confidence and positive emotions in women. She believes that these qualities act as timeless medicine, empowering them to grow stronger and more resilient. Through her own example, Oksana Hankevich guides and supports other women who unfortunately find themselves in similar circumstances, helping them discover their inner strength.

ORGANISERS

Lifeline Express is a non-profit organization established in 2022 with the sole purpose of providing aid to the victims of Russian aggression in Ukraine.

Our team consists of passionate individuals dedicated to helping those in need, particularly during times of crisis.

We are deeply moved by the immense suffering endured by the people of Ukraine and are determined to make a positive impact in any way possible.

We recognise that even small acts of kindness can significantly improve the lives of those facing hardship and as a British charitable group, we are committed to collaborating with smaller charities on the ground in Ukraine, ensuring that aid reaches often overlooked areas that large charities may miss. Our focus is on targeted assistance that addresses specific needs.

Our focus is on targeted assistance that addresses specific needs. We achieve this through our network of dedicated young Ukrainian volunteer drivers and our connections with smaller schools and hospitals in remote regions.

We take great pride in presenting our Live Operatic Performance, inspired by the unwavering dedication and heroism of the Ukrainian people, witnessed during our work on the ground. It fills us with immense pride knowing that all the funds we raise will be dedicated to supporting victims of the war, including Ukrainian artists who have been displaced as a result of the aggressor's actions.

We are thankful for the support and collaboration of British-Ukrainian Aid, an established charity actively engaged in providing aid and support to war victims in Ukraine through various initiatives.

Our collective efforts aim to contribute to a more peaceful and just world for everyone affected by this ongoing conflict.

PROCEEDS

We would like to highlight that some of the funds raised from our event's sponsors will be used to cover some essential expenses for the successful execution of the event. Finally, a portion of the funds will be allocated towards renting the church venue where the event will take place. By sponsoring our event, your company will be providing direct support to the local arts and culture scene in Ukraine, while also ensuring that the event is executed to the highest possible standard. We aim to keep the fees associated with setting up the event to a minimum so that the majority of the proceeds generated can be directed towards selected charities operating in Ukraine.

Your sponsorship will not only help to promote the arts and culture scene in Ukraine but will also help the rehabilitation of the victims of war. We believe that supporting these charities is crucial in creating a positive impact on the community and promoting social and economic development in Ukraine. By sponsoring our event, your company will not only have the opportunity to gain exposure and network with key players in the Ukrainian business community, but you will also be contributing towards a worthy cause. The impact of your contribution will be felt by those who benefit from the services provided by the chosen charities, and your generosity will be recognized and appreciated by the wider community.
